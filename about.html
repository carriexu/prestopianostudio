About Us

Presto Piano Studio is dedicated to nurturing musical talent and fostering a deep appreciation for the piano. Located in the serene Highland Park neighborhood of Pittsburgh, PA, we offer personalized piano instruction to students of all ages and skill levels.

Junyi (Jimmy) Liu - Founder & Principal Instructor

Jimmy Liu is a classical pianist with a passion for teaching. His musical journey began at age 6 when he moved to Pittsburgh from Chang Chun, China. Jimmy's talent was evident from an early age, as he found much success in piano competitions while growing up, including winning the Duquesne Young Artists, PPTA, MTNA PA State, Pittsburgh Civic Orchestra, and many other competitions.

After high school, Jimmy pursued his passion at Carnegie Mellon University, where he received both his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in piano performance. He also earned certificates in piano tuning and piano pedagogy, broadening his expertise in the field.

Today, Jimmy maintains a thriving piano studio with over 50 students, ranging in age from 5 to 75. His students have achieved remarkable success, winning competitions, performing at prestigious venues like Carnegie Hall in NYC and Heinz Hall in Pittsburgh, and even playing with orchestras.

In addition to his private studio, Jimmy is an active educator in the Pittsburgh music community. He teaches for Carnegie Mellon University and the Ellis School, showcasing his commitment to music education at various levels. Jimmy's expertise extends beyond teaching. He is also a skilled piano tuner, providing his services to Carnegie Mellon University, Chatham University, and over 550 private clients.
